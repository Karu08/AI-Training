FINANCE POLICY of NemoCompany

1. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Employees may claim reimbursement for business-related expenses.
Eligible expenses include:
- Travel expenses
- Meals during business travel
- Office supplies
All reimbursement claims must:
- Include valid receipts
- Be submitted within 30 days of the expense
Reimbursements are processed within 10 business days after approval.

2. PAYROLL PROCESSING
Payroll is processed on the 28th of every month.
If the 28th falls on a weekend or holiday, payroll will be processed on the previous business day.
Salary slips are available in the payroll portal.

3. BUDGET REPORTS
Monthly and quarterly budget reports are maintained by the finance team.
Reports can be accessed through the company’s SharePoint finance folder.
Access requests must be approved by the finance manager.

4. TAX DEDUCTIONS
Statutory deductions include:
- Income tax
- Provident fund contributions
- Professional tax (if applicable)
Employees can download tax statements from the payroll system.

5. ADVANCES & LOANS
Employees may request salary advances or loans.
Requests must be approved by:
- Reporting manager
- Finance department
Repayment schedules will be communicated upon approval.

6. FINANCE CONTACT
All finance-related queries should be submitted via the finance helpdesk portal.
Urgent payroll issues should be flagged as “Payroll Priority.”
